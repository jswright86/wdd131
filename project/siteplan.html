2. Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide a comprehensive guide for woodworking enthusiasts of all skill levels. It will feature articles, tips, and project ideas to help users improve their woodworking skills and inspire them to take on new projects.

The color scheme for the site will be earthy tones, primarily using shades of brown, green, and beige to reflect the natural materials used in woodworking.
